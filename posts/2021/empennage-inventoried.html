Empennage Inventoried

📅 October 27, 2021 📁 Empennage

It finally happened on the 25th of October 2021 that my kit arrived. I took it out to the hangar the next day with the help of Anil, who has a bigger car so the larger box would fit.

There were two items missing: a small rib and the USB plans. I have asked Van's for those. On the whole the kit was well packaged and arrived in very good condition.

The inventorying took 3.5 hours. Now the real hard part starts — and many thousand hours to go.
