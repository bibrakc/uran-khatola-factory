Finished the Practice Flap Kit

📅 October 20, 2021 📁 Planning ⏱ 10 hrs logged

This flap practice kit is excellent in terms of providing the necessary skills and experience of most of the build process.

Completed practice flap.

Completed practice flap.

I went with a red oxide color primer just to figure out a primer scheme and color for the actual build. I think this is overkill, especially for parts that will be covered inside. I will from now on use a basic self-etch primer by Rust-Oleum. Maybe later in areas that are open, like the fuselage, I might go for this or a white color.

The process. There were some mistakes made and I tried to fix them. After all, it's a practice project for a reason.
