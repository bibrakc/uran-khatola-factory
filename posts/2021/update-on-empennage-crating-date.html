Update on Empennage Crating Date

📅 May 12, 2021 📁 Planning ⏱ 1 hr logged

Crate Date Letter from Van's.

Crate Date Letter from Van's.

Received a letter from Van's that I need to sign and send back. The purpose of the letter is twofold: first, to confirm that my kit is in the queue; and second, to have me look at the complete order and attest that everything looks OK.

The expected crating date is September 2021. This would mean I should be expecting it at my doorstep maybe later in September 2021.

Now I will just patiently wait and use the time to better educate myself about the blueprints (plans) that I have also ordered and should be coming anytime soon.
